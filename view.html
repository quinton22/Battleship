Instructions

How to Play:

  1. One player start by dragging ships onto the board. Once all the ships are on the board, press done.
  2. The next player should then place their ships.
  3. The person to go first is chosen at random. They will click on a space that they think the opponent placed a battleship. It will either result in a hit or a miss.
  4. The other player will then take their turn.
  5. Once a player hits all parts of a ship, it sinks. And once a player sinks all of the opponent's ships, the game is won!
  6. Pull the bar on the right to display more information during gameplay.
